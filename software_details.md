# Workforce Intelligence Platform

## Overview
The **Workforce Intelligence Platform** is a state-of-the-art SaaS application tailored for modern HR and Engineering leadership. It transcends basic employee tracking by leveraging Artificial Intelligence to transform raw personnel data into actionable, predictive insights. The platform enables organizations to dynamically discover skill gaps, automatically assemble optimized project teams, predict employee flight risks, and strategically map the future of their workforce using generative AI.

---

## 🛠 Technology Stack

### Frontend (Client)
- **Framework:** React (v18.3)
- **Build Tool:** Vite
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS, clsx, tailwind-merge
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
- **Data Visualization:** Recharts (for analytics and dynamic charts), React Flow (for interactive mappings)
- **Icons:** Lucide React

### Backend (Server)
- **Runtime:** Node.js
- **Framework:** Express.js
- **Database & Authentication:** Supabase
- **AI Integration:** Anthropic AI SDK (Claude models), Groq SDK (High-performance inference)
- **File Processing:** Multer (file uploads), pdf-parse (PDF resume parsing)

## 🚀 Core Platform Modules & Features

### 1. Executive Intelligence Dashboard
Provides a real-time, comprehensive view of organizational capability and health.
- **Dynamic KPIs:** Tracks Total Headcount, Average Experience, Average Performance, and calculates a unified **Workforce Capability Score**.
- **Predictive Analytics:** AI-forecasted insights including Skill Decay Risk, Bench Utilization Forecasts, Attrition Risk, Project Success Probability, and Hiring Pipeline Health.
- **Organization Skill Mapping:** Interactive radar charts displaying average proficiency across top technical capabilities.
- **Talent Spotlight:** Automatically highlights top-rated capability experts and specialists inside the organization.

### 2. The Employee Workspace
A centralized hub for exploring talent and generating personalized career growth trajectories.
- **AI Resume Intelligence:** Upload a candidate's PDF or text resume for the AI to automatically parse, categorize, and generate a structured profile complete with skills, experience, and proficiency scores.
- **AI Learning Roadmaps:** Generates customized, step-by-step upskilling roadmaps tailored to current skills and future tech stack targets.
- **AI Promotion Readiness Index:** Evaluates promotion readiness based on experience, performance, and skill breadth, providing a score, reasoning, and specific improvement areas.

### 3. Project Staffing Engine
Calculates the mathematically optimal team for any given project.
- **Intelligent Assembly:** Input project details (skills, size, duration) and the AI nominates a primary team and backup candidates.
- **Competency Coverage Mapping:** Visualizes how the recommended team covers required skills and identifies any remaining gaps.
- **Skill Match Scoring:** Calculates a precise "fit" percentage for every candidate based on technical overlap, experience, and past performance.

---

## 🤖 Advanced Enterprise AI Features

### 4. Semantic Skill Search
- **Search with Intent:** Search using natural language (e.g., *"needs someone good with cloud and microservices"*) instead of rigid keywords. The platform's AI Vector Engine semantically maps requests to the right candidates.

### 5. Cross-Project Conflict Resolver
- **Eliminate Over-Allocation:** Continuously monitors assignments to detect double-booked or over-allocated resources.
- **AI Resolution Engine:** Generates weighted resolution options (e.g., Split Allocation, Alternative Employee, Upskill Replacement, Delay Project) ranked by Business Impact, Delivery Risk, Skill Match, and Cost.

### 6. Capability Risk & SPOF Detection
- **Single Point of Failure (SPOF) Analysis:** Instantly identifies critical skills held by only a single employee.
- **Flight Risk Monitoring:** Flags employees posing a high capability risk if they leave, offering mitigation strategies and alternative internal candidates.

### 7. AI Succession Simulator
- **Scenario Modeling:** Simulates an employee's Promotion, Resignation, or Department Transfer.
- **Forecast Impact:** Recalculates organizational capability, highlights impacted projects, identifies lost skills, and recommends internal successors (Ready Now, Ready 1-2 Yrs, Needs Upskilling).

### 8. Context-Aware AI ChatBot
- **Talk to Your Workforce Data:** An embedded generative AI assistant that understands the entire workforce and project landscape.
- **Strategic Queries:** Answer complex questions like *"Who is qualified to lead our upcoming AI project?"* or *"Which department lacks cloud skills?"*

## 💡 The Value Proposition
- **Maximize Utilization:** Ensure the right people are on the right projects at the right time.
- **Retain Top Talent:** Proactively identify flight risks and provide clear, AI-guided promotion pathways.
- **De-Risk Delivery:** Eliminate single points of failure and resolve resource conflicts proactively.
- **Future-Proof the Org:** Compare current capabilities against industry standards and generate targeted upskilling recommendations.
